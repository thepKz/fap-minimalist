# Privacy Policy for FAP Minimal Skin

Effective date: 2026-04-01

FAP Minimal Skin is a Chrome extension that customizes the visual appearance of pages on `https://fap.fpt.edu.vn/`.

## Data We Collect

FAP Minimal Skin does **not** collect, transmit, or sell personal data of any kind.

The extension only stores one local preference in your browser:

- **Theme toggle state** (enabled/disabled)

This preference is saved using `chrome.storage.local` and is used solely to remember your selected visual mode across browser sessions. It never leaves your device.

## Data Sharing

We do not share any user data with third parties. No analytics, no tracking, no remote logging.

## Remote Servers

FAP Minimal Skin does **not** make any network requests. It does not send your browsing content, form data, account data, academic records, or personal information to any external server.

All data processing happens entirely locally within your browser.

## Permissions Usage

| Permission | Why it is needed |
|---|---|
| `storage` | Save and read your local theme preference (on/off) |
| `activeTab` | Apply the visual theme to the currently active FAP tab |

No other permissions are requested or used.

## User Consent

By installing and enabling FAP Minimal Skin, you consent to the local storage of your theme preference as described above. You may withdraw consent at any time by disabling or uninstalling the extension, which will remove all stored data.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy in future releases. Any update will be published on this page with a revised effective date.
