# Privacy Policy for FAP Minimal Skin

Effective date: 2026-04-01

FAP Minimal Skin is a Chrome extension that customizes the visual appearance of pages on `https://fap.fpt.edu.vn/`.

## Data We Collect

FAP Minimal Skin does not collect, transmit, or sell personal data.

The extension only stores one local preference in your browser:

- Theme toggle state (enabled/disabled)

This preference is saved using Chrome Extension Storage (`chrome.storage.local`) and is used only to remember your selected visual mode.

## Data Sharing

We do not share any user data with third parties.

## Remote Servers

FAP Minimal Skin does not send your browsing content, form data, account data, or personal information to any external server.

## Permissions Usage

- `storage`: save and read local theme preference.
- `activeTab`: support extension interaction on the currently active tab.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy in future releases. Any update will be published on this page with a revised effective date.
